# Public Data Options

## Option A: Electricity Zones Brief
- Format: Word document
- Topic: Swedish electricity zones and municipality mapping
- Use in class: file grounding and retrieval quality comparison

## Option B: Solar Capacity Spreadsheet
- Format: Excel workbook or CSV
- Topic: installed solar panel capacity over time or by geography
- Use in class: tabular grounding and simple filtering tasks

## Option C: Electricity Price Time Series
- Format: Excel workbook or CSV
- Topic: spot prices over time
- Use in class: trend questions, summaries, and comparison prompts
